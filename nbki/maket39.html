National Bureau of Credit Histories Joint Stock Company

National Bureau of Credit Histories Joint Stock Company (NBCH JSC) is the largest company in the Russian Federation credit history market. It was established on March 30, 2005 and is acting under the Federal Law on Credit Histories.

NBCH JSC is licensed by the Federal Service for Technical and Expert Control of Russian Federation for the technical protection of confidential information. NBCH JSC is registered with the State Register of Credit History Bureaus under No. 077-00003-002.

The database of credit histories of individuals and legal entities has been created by NBCH JSC as a result of cooperation with more than 4000 organizations in all regions of Russia, which is largely superior to similar figures for all other credit bureaus of the country taken together. Each of the top 10 banks of Russia is a partner of the Bureau, as well as each one of the top 100.

NBCH JSC pays great importance to the development of partner relations, joint search for solutions to the issues of technological development of business processes. The Bureau develops long-term and mutually beneficial relations with our partners, such as TransUnion (USA), CRIF (Italy), and Fair Isaac (USA), which are the world leaders in the development of information solutions offering their clients the most up-to-date, advanced, and coordinated technologies. Thanks to this cooperation, NBCH JSC is ahead of other credit bureaus in terms of the quality of our services, which correspond to the highest international standards.
